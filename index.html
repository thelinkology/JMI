What We Do

Comprehensive Engineering Solutions

01
Fire Protection Systems

We design, supply, install, and maintain highly reliable fire protection systems that safeguard lives, assets, and infrastructure. Our solutions are engineered to meet stringent safety codes and performance standards, ensuring rapid response and effective fire suppression in mission-critical environments.

Value DeliveredEnhanced life safety, code compliance, asset protection, and reduced operational risk.
02
Plumbing & Sanitary Systems

Our plumbing and sanitary solutions are built for durability, efficiency, and long-term operational performance. We provide end-to-end system design, installation, and maintenance for commercial, institutional, and industrial properties.

Value DeliveredEfficient water flow systems, improved sanitation reliability, and reduced maintenance downtime.
03
Detection & Alarm Systems

We provide advanced fire detection and alarm systems that enable early warning, fast incident response, and comprehensive building safety integration. Our systems are designed to ensure immediate notification and protection in critical spaces.

Value DeliveredEarly detection, improved emergency response, and enhanced occupant safety.
04
Pumps for All Applications

JMI supplies and services high-performance pumps for a wide range of applications including fire protection, water transfer, drainage, pressure boosting, and industrial operations.

Value DeliveredReliable flow management, consistent system pressure, and optimized operational efficiency.
05
Automatic Fire & Jockey Pump Controllers

Our intelligent control systems are designed to automate the operation, monitoring, and reliability of fire pumps and jockey pumps, ensuring system readiness at all times.

Value DeliveredAutomated reliability, pressure consistency, and reduced system failure risks.
06
Electric Motors & Control Centers

We provide expert installation, servicing, and maintenance of electric motors and control centers essential to industrial and building operations.

Value DeliveredImproved equipment performance, operational continuity, and extended asset lifespan.
07
Generating Sets & Allied Equipment

Our standby power solutions are engineered to provide dependable backup power during outages and operational interruptions, ensuring uninterrupted business continuity.

Value DeliveredPower resilience, business continuity, and critical infrastructure protection.
08
Generator Control Panels

We deliver advanced control panel solutions for generator automation, synchronization, load management, and real-time monitoring.

Value DeliveredEfficient power distribution, seamless generator performance, and intelligent system control.
09
General Rewinding Services

Our professional rewinding services restore the efficiency and functionality of motors and electrical equipment, extending operational life and reducing replacement costs.

Value DeliveredCost efficiency, restored performance, and prolonged equipment lifespan.
10
Preventive Maintenance Programs

Our preventive maintenance solutions are structured to proactively identify risks, minimize equipment downtime, and preserve system integrity over the long term.

Value DeliveredReduced downtime, improved system reliability, and long-term cost savings.
